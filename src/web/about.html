What we do

Code Labs is a consultant company built by consultants. With our years of experience working as consultants in system development, operations and infrastructure engineering, we know what a consultant need from its employer to reach peak performance. Freedom and more responsibility.

Code Labs is a company built on that idea - We put our consultants first. We know our consultants can deliver quality results because of this. We have many years of experience in system development and managing large scale infrastructure for our customers in many fields like gaming, retail and online streaming.
